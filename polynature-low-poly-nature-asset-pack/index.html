PolyNature – Low Poly Nature Asset Pack

Pre-made assets in the form of prefabs, allowing for hassle-free integration into your game project. With this convenient featudy game developers, designers, and artists of varying skill levels can efficiently create captivating forest environments without. dealing with complicated setups. This simplified process ensures that creators can focus on their vision and execute it smoothly without any technical roadblocks.

Additionally, the variety of trees included in this set ensures that you have a wide range of options to choose from. Whether you’re looking to create a dense forest or a sparse one, you’ll have the flexibility to choose the right trees for your scene. Furthermore, the availability of different seasons ensures that you can create a dynamic environment that changes with the passing of time. Overall, the Low Poly Trees Pack is an excellent asset for game developers looking to create immersive and visually appealing environments for their games.
 
PACKAGE CONTAINS
44 unique low poly tree prefabs:
  • 2 Acacia Trees
  • 1 Apple Trees
  • 4 Dead Trees
  • 3 Palm Trees
  • 6 Pine Trees
  • 2 Mushrooms
  • 10 Simple Trees
  • 3 Cactus
  • 3 Flowers
  • 2 Plants
  • 4 Grasses
  • 2 Bushes
  • 2 Bushes and rocks
5 unique low poly wood prefabs:
  • 1 Stumps
  • 4 Logs
Also, you get 7 Bonus prefabs:
  • 5 Rocks
  • 2 Mountain
Also PolyNature – Low Poly Nature Asset Pack includes 27 materials that are utilized for all the prefabs mentioned earlier. Additionally, by using the “Autumn Leaves” material, it is possible to create Autumn Trees with ease.
